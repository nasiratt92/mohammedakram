About

After leaving school I gave 15 years to the construction, civil engineering and local authority combined, being a technician for the highways and engineering. On a one year professional career break, my awareness of the digital world increased and led to the discovery of how far the industry has evolved.

I wanted to break into this field to become a full stack developer. I found General Assembly to be the most suitable place for me to develop my skill set.

On 14th September 2018 I completed the 12 weeks Web Development Immersive programe.
