About

Gave 15 years to the construction, civil engineering and local authority combined, being a technician for the highways and engineering. On a one year professional career break, my awareness of the digital world increased and led to the discovery of how far the industry has evolved. I wanted to break into this field to become a full stack developer. I found General Assembly to be the most suitable place for me to develop my skill set. I love the idea of working in a agile environment, with a good team. I would like to work with people to jointly host brainstorming and trying to keep it useful and fun. I really enjoy the professionalism and depth of this industry.
